Pangaea

Our continents were formed from Pangaea; a supercontinent that was formed some 335 million years ago. This time period, between 280 million and 230 million years before present, was known as the late Paleozoic to early Mesozoic Era. The name “Pangaea” is from the Greek word ‘Pan’ which means all or nature, and Gaia which refers to Earth and our seas also used to be one, Panthalassa.
