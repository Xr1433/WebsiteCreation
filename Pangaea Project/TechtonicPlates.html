Techtonic Plates

What are techtonic plates?

Techtonic Plates are massive plates of rock the make up Earth's crust. There are 10 large plates and many other minor ones, and they rest on a layer of the mantle called the asthenosphere.

Techtonic plates are the creators of the landscapes, they form many different features in Earth's landscape including Mount Everest! They are also the creators of earthquakes.
